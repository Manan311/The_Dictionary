The app’s purpose is to define or translate the English-language terminology we use in the computer programming courses in the School of SDDS, for use by all students, whether or not English is a student’s first language.

WEB API: https://hidden-waters-14346.herokuapp.com/

English

Get All English Term: api/termEnglish

Get One English Term By Id: api/termEnglish/:id

Get One English Term By Word: api/termEnglish/word/:word

Add New English Term: api/termEnglish

Add Definition For English Term: /api/termEnglish/:id/add-defintion

Increment Yes Counter For English Term: /api/termEnglish/yes/:id

Increment No Counter For English Term: /api/termEnglish/no/:id

Increment Likes Counter For English Terms Definition: /api/termEnglish/likes/:id

Delete a English Term: /api/delete/termEnglish/:id
